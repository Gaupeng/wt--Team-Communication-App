About The Project This is TaskMan! A simple web-application to discuss and work on projects with your team. Assign tasks, get resources, and chat with your team on this app. Technologies used in the project:
Frontend: Angular and Ionic Framework
Backend: Firebase, Flask
Web-Tech Technologies: Rest API, Periodic Refresh & Multistage Download
